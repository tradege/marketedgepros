# How to Request a Withdrawal

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

---

Requesting a withdrawal of your profits from your MarketEdgePros funded account is a simple and straightforward process.

## 1. Eligibility for Withdrawal

- **First Withdrawal:** You are eligible to request your first withdrawal 30 calendar days after you place your first trade on your funded account.
- **Subsequent Withdrawals:** After your first withdrawal, you can request subsequent withdrawals on-demand, at any time, as long as you have profits in your account.

---

## 2. Go to Your Trader Dashboard

Log in to your MarketEdgePros account and navigate to your main trader dashboard.

---

## 3. Click on "Request Payout"

You will see a "Request Payout" or "Withdrawal" button on your dashboard. Click this to begin the process.

---

## 4. Enter the Withdrawal Amount

Specify the amount of profit you wish to withdraw. You can withdraw all or part of your available profits.

- **[Minimum Withdrawal Amount](/support/minimum-withdrawal-amount)**

---

## 5. Choose Your Payout Method

Select your preferred method for receiving your funds. We offer a variety of options:

- **Bank Transfer (via SWIFT)**
- **Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT)**
- **PayPal**

- **[Payment Methods Accepted](/support/payment-methods-accepted)**

---

## 6. Submit Your Request

Review your withdrawal request to ensure all details are correct, and then click "Submit".

Our team will be notified of your request and will begin processing it.

- **[Withdrawal Processing Times](/support/withdrawal-processing-times)**

---

### Questions?

If you have any issues with requesting a withdrawal, our support team is ready to help.

- **[Contact Support](/contact)**
