# Payment Methods Accepted

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

---

We offer a variety of convenient and secure methods for both paying for your challenge and receiving your profit withdrawals.

## For Challenge Payments

When you purchase a MarketEdgePros challenge, you can pay using:

### 1. Credit & Debit Cards
- **Providers:** Visa, MasterCard, American Express
- **Speed:** Instant
- **Security:** Processed through Stripe, a leading global payment processor.

### 2. Cryptocurrencies
- **Coins:** Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT (ERC-20), and other major cryptocurrencies.
- **Speed:** Depends on blockchain confirmation times (usually 10-60 minutes).
- **Security:** Processed through Coinbase Commerce.

---

## For Profit Withdrawals

When you request a payout of your profits, you can choose from the following methods:

### 1. Bank Transfer
- **Method:** International Wire Transfer (SWIFT)
- **Speed:** 3-7 business days, depending on your bank and location.
- **Fees:** Your bank may charge a fee for incoming international wires. MarketEdgePros does not charge a fee for this method.

### 2. Cryptocurrency
- **Coins:** Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and USDT (ERC-20).
- **Speed:** Very fast (typically under 1 hour).
- **Fees:** You will need to cover the blockchain network fees, which are usually very low.

### 3. PayPal
- **Method:** Direct transfer to your PayPal account.
- **Speed:** Almost instant.
- **Fees:** PayPal may charge a fee for receiving funds.

---

> **Important:** Please ensure that your payout details (e.g., bank account number or crypto wallet address) are correct. We are not responsible for funds sent to an incorrect address provided by you.
