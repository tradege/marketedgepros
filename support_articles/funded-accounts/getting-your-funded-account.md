## 1. Account Verification

Once you have successfully passed all the objectives of your evaluation, our team will automatically be notified.

- We will conduct a final review of your trading activity to ensure all rules were followed.
- This process is usually completed within **24 business hours**.

---

## 2. Sign Your Trader Agreement

After your account is verified, you will receive an email with a link to sign your official Trader Agreement. This is a legal document that outlines the terms and conditions of our partnership.

- Please read the agreement carefully.
- You can sign it digitally online.

---

## 3. Receive Your Funded Account Credentials

Once you have signed the agreement, we will create your live funded account. You will receive a new set of MT5 credentials via email.

- **New Login & Password:** You will get a new account number and password for the live server.
- **Server:** You will be connecting to our live server (e.g., `MarketEdgePros-Live`).

This process is also typically completed within **24 business hours** after you sign the agreement.

---

## 4. Start Trading and Earning

Your funded account is now ready to go! You can log in to MT5 with your new credentials and start trading.

### Key Things to Remember:

- **No Profit Target:** There are no profit targets on funded accounts. You can request a withdrawal after your first profitable trade.
- **Same Loss Limits:** The daily and overall loss limits still apply, calculated from your initial funded account balance.
- **First Payout:** You are eligible for your first payout 30 days after your first trade on the funded account. After that, you can request payouts on-demand.
