# Changing Your Password

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

---

Regularly changing your password is a good security practice. This guide shows you how to change the password for your MarketEdgePros dashboard and your MT5 trading account.

## Changing Your Dashboard Password

This is the password you use to log in to the MarketEdgePros website.

1.  **Log in** to your dashboard.
2.  Navigate to the **"Security"** or **"Account Settings"** section.
3.  Click on **"Change Password"**.
4.  You will be asked to enter your **current password** and then your **new password** twice.
5.  Click **"Save"**.

### Forgot Your Dashboard Password?

If you have forgotten your dashboard password, you can reset it:

1.  On the login page, click the **"Forgot Password?"** link.
2.  Enter your registered email address.
3.  You will receive an email with a link to create a new password.


## Changing Your MT5 Password

This is the password for your trading account on the MT5 platform.

1.  Open your MT5 platform and log in to your trading account.
2.  Go to **Tools > Options**.
3.  Select the **"Server"** tab.
4.  Click the **"Change"** button next to the password field.
5.  Enter your **current MT5 password**.
6.  Select **"Change master password"**.
7.  Enter your **new password** twice.
8.  Click **"OK"**.

> **Important:** We do not have access to your MT5 password. If you forget it, we will need to reset it for you. Please contact support if you lose your MT5 password.
