# Updating Your Profile

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

---

Keeping your profile information up-to-date is important for security and communication purposes. This guide shows you how to update your personal details in your MarketEdgePros dashboard.

## Step 1: Log In to Your Dashboard

Go to the MarketEdgePros website and log in to your account using your email and password.

## Step 2: Navigate to "Profile" or "Account Settings"

Once logged in, look for a menu item or icon that says "Profile," "My Account," or "Settings." Click on it to access your account details.

## Step 3: Edit Your Information

You will see the personal information associated with your account. You can typically edit the following:

- **Name:** (May require support intervention for legal name changes)
- **Address:** Your residential address.
- **Phone Number:** Your contact phone number.

Click the "Edit" or "Save" button next to the information you wish to change and enter the new details.

> **Note:** For security reasons, you cannot change your registered email address yourself. If you need to change your email, please contact our support team.

## Step 4: Save Your Changes

After making your updates, be sure to click the **"Save Changes"** or **"Update Profile"** button to ensure your new information is saved.

---

### Why is this important?

- **Payouts:** We need your correct information to process your profit withdrawals.
- **Communication:** We use your email and phone number to send you important account updates.
- **Security:** Keeping your details current helps us verify your identity if you ever need to recover your account.
