# Making Your First Payment at MarketEdgePros

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

---

## 1. Select Your Challenge

Once you have chosen your desired challenge and account size on our **[Programs](/programs)** page, click the **"Get Started"** button. You will be redirected to our secure checkout page.

---

## 2. Choose Your Payment Method

We accept a variety of payment methods to make it easy for you to get started. 

### Credit/Debit Cards
- We accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express.
- This is the fastest and most common payment method.

### Cryptocurrencies
- We also accept payments in various cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and USDT (Tether).
- Payments are processed securely through our partner, Coinbase Commerce.

> **Note:** If you pay with cryptocurrency, please ensure you send the exact amount to the correct wallet address. Transactions on the blockchain are irreversible.

---

## 3. Apply a Discount Code (Optional)

If you have a discount code, you can apply it in the "Discount Code" field on the checkout page. The discount will be applied to your total amount.

Keep an eye on our social media channels and blog for special promotions and discounts!

---

## 4. Complete the Payment

Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your payment.

- For credit card payments, you will need to enter your card details and billing address.
- For crypto payments, you will be given a unique wallet address and a QR code to send the funds to.

Once your payment is confirmed, you will receive an email with your trading account credentials and instructions on how to get started.

---

## 5. What Happens Next?

After a successful payment, you will receive two emails:

1.  **Payment Confirmation:** A receipt for your purchase.
2.  **Account Credentials:** Your MT5 login, password, and server details.

You can then proceed to **[Setting Up MT5](/support/setup-mt5)** and start your challenge!

---

### Payment Issues?

If you encounter any issues during the payment process, please try the following:

- **Double-check your card details:** Ensure the card number, expiry date, and CVC are correct.
- **Contact your bank:** Sometimes, banks may block international transactions for security reasons.
- **Try a different payment method:** If your card is declined, you can try using a different card or cryptocurrency.

If you still have trouble, our support team is here to help.
