# Setting Up MT5 with MarketEdgePros

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

---

## 1. Download MetaTrader 5 (MT5)

MetaTrader 5 is the platform you will use to trade on your MarketEdgePros account. If you don't have it installed, you can download it for free from the official MetaTrader website.

- **[Download MT5 for Windows](https://www.metatrader5.com/en/download)**
- **[Download MT5 for Mac](https://www.metatrader5.com/en/download)**
- **[Download MT5 for Linux](https://www.metatrader5.com/en/download)**

MT5 is also available for iOS and Android devices from the App Store and Google Play Store.

---

## 2. Find Your Account Credentials

After purchasing your challenge, you will receive an email with your MT5 account credentials. This email will contain:

- **Account Number (Login):** A unique number for your trading account.
- **Password:** Your trading account password.
- **Server:** The name of the server you need to connect to (e.g., `MarketEdgePros-Demo`).

Keep this email in a safe place.

---

## 3. Log in to Your MT5 Account

Once you have MT5 installed, follow these steps to log in:

1.  Open the MT5 terminal.
2.  Go to **File > Login to Trade Account**.
3.  Enter your **Account Number** in the "Login" field.
4.  Enter your **Password** in the "Password" field.
5.  Select the correct **Server** from the dropdown list.
6.  Click **"OK"**.

If the login is successful, you will hear a confirmation sound, and you will see your account balance and trade history in the "Terminal" window.

> **Troubleshooting:** If you can't log in, double-check that you have entered the correct login, password, and server. Passwords are case-sensitive.

---

## 4. Familiarize Yourself with the Platform

If you are new to MT5, we recommend spending some time getting familiar with the platform. Here are some key windows to know:

- **Market Watch:** Shows a list of available trading instruments and their prices.
- **Navigator:** Allows you to manage your accounts, indicators, and Expert Advisors (EAs).
- **Chart Window:** Displays the price chart for a selected instrument.
- **Terminal:** Shows your account balance, trade history, open positions, and journal.

---

## 5. Start Trading!

You are now ready to start trading on your MarketEdgePros challenge account. 

Remember to always follow our **[Trading Rules](/support/trading-rules)** and practice good risk management.

Good luck!
