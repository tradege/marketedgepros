**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

## 1. Understand the Different Challenge Types

MarketEdgePros offers several types of challenges to suit different trading styles and experience levels. Hereâ€™s a breakdown of our main programs:

### One-Phase Challenge
- **Goal:** Reach a profit target in a single trading phase.
- **Best for:** Confident traders who want to get funded quickly.
- **Profit Target:** 10%

### Two-Phase Challenge
- **Goal:** Pass two consecutive evaluation phases with lower profit targets.
- **Best for:** Traders who prefer a more conservative approach.
- **Profit Targets:** 8% (Phase 1) and 5% (Phase 2)

### Three-Phase Challenge
- **Goal:** A longer evaluation with even lower profit targets.
- **Best for:** New traders or those who want to build consistency.
- **Profit Targets:** 6% (Phase 1), 6% (Phase 2), 6% (Phase 3)

### Instant Funding
- **Goal:** Skip the evaluation and start trading with real capital immediately.
- **Best for:** Experienced traders with a proven track record.
- **Note:** Comes with stricter drawdown rules.

## 2. Select Your Account Size

We offer a wide range of account sizes, from **$10,000 to $400,000**. 

When choosing your account size, consider the following:

- **Your Experience Level:** If you are new to prop trading, it may be wise to start with a smaller account ($10K or $25K) to get comfortable with our rules.
- **Your Trading Strategy:** Does your strategy require a larger account to manage risk effectively?
- **The Cost:** Larger accounts have a higher one-time fee.

> **Remember:** You can always scale your account later! Consistent, profitable traders are eligible for account scaling up to $400,000.

## 3. Review the Trading Rules

All our challenges share a common set of simple, transparent rules. Before you start, make sure you understand:

- **Profit Target:** The percentage of profit you need to make.
- **Max Daily Loss:** The maximum amount you can lose in a single day (usually 5%).
- **Max Overall Loss:** The maximum total drawdown your account can have (usually 10-12%).
- **Minimum Trading Days:** The minimum number of days you need to trade (usually 5-10 days).
- **Prohibited Strategies:** We do not allow high-risk strategies like martingale or grid trading.

For a complete list of rules, please see our **[Trading Rules](/support/trading-rules)** page.
