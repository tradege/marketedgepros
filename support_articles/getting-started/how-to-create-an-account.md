# How to Create an Account at MarketEdgePros

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

---

## 1. Visit the Registration Page

To begin, navigate to the MarketEdgePros homepage and click the **"Get Funded"** button in the top-right corner. You can also click any of the "Get Started" buttons on our program cards.

This will take you to our secure registration page.

---

## 2. Fill in Your Personal Details

You will be asked to provide the following information:

- **Full Name:** Your legal first and last name.
- **Email Address:** A valid email address that you have access to. This will be used for all communication.
- **Password:** A strong, unique password. We recommend using a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
- **Country of Residence:** Select your country from the dropdown list.

> **Security Note:** We take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. For more information, please see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).

---

## 3. Verify Your Email Address

After submitting the registration form, you will receive an email with a verification link. 

1.  Check your inbox for an email from `support@marketedgepros.com`.
2.  Click the **"Verify Email"** link in the email.
3.  This will confirm your email address and activate your account.

If you don't see the email, please check your spam or junk folder.

---

## 4. Complete Your Profile

Once your email is verified, you will be redirected to your new dashboard. 

Before you can start a challenge, you will need to complete your profile by providing some additional information for KYC (Know Your Customer) purposes. This may include:

- **Phone Number**
- **Date of Birth**
- **Full Address**

---

## 5. Choose Your Challenge

Congratulations! Your account is now active. 

You can now browse our different funding programs and choose the challenge that best suits your trading style and goals.

- **[Choosing the Right Challenge](/support/choose-challenge)**
- **[Making Your First Payment](/support/first-payment)**
